MARITEX

Singapore

History

Maritex Pte. Ltd. Is a specialized workshop which provides quality repair and maintenance work for various machineries in marine and offshore industries. The company also provides supply of all kinds of equipment and material required for foreign going and offshore vessels. Skilled and professional engineers and technicians will be performing technical tasks in an efficient way with minimum time and cost.

Our Vision and Mission

We believe in long term relations with the client and constant drive to meet their needs and expectations.

Activities

  • Repair and maintenance:
    • Repair and overhauling of all types of engines including slow, medium and high speed engines
    • Repair and overhauling of all types of pumps, air compressors etc.
    • Supply and necessary spares as required for the overhauling
    • Providing rider crew for voyage repair
    • Specialized in all kinds of marine diesel engine, especially MAN B&W Wartsila engine
    • Specialized in lubricating system for MC, MC-C, ME Engine and spare supply, Alpha and mechanical lubricator
    • Specialized in pneumatic system maintenance and spare supply
    • Special repairs like camshaft replacement, damage cam installation, main chain related work.
    • Crankshaft replacement and timing adjustment for distributer, lubricater, fuel cam, exhaust cam
  • Logistics and Supply:
    • Fabrication of equipment and erection of plants
    • Supply of various hardware, electrical and mechanical equipment
    • Clearing and freight forwarding of project equipment (air and sea)
    • Rental/sales arrangement of various equipment like cranes, fork lift, pay loader, trailers, barges, excavator, bulldozer etc.
    • Supply of air compressor (Ingersoil-Rand, Nuovo Pigone etc.)
    • Supply of piping and pipe fittings (ball v/v, check v/v, blowdown v/v, all are UL (under writer lab) certified for oil and gas application
    • High pressure fittings such as Grayloc, swagelock etc.
    • Supply of flue gas separator
    • Marine and offshore generators for oil and gas companies
    • Electrical grounding items, distribution panel boards, cables
    • HVAC system
    • Drilling tools, drilling liquids, drilling support
    • Supply of hatch covers and hatch coamings
    • Supply of firefighting, lifesaving and testing equipment
    • Project based logistic support provider including transportation of equipment from port to project site and vice versa
    • Supplying skilled manpower of various categories (engineers, wielders, crane and excavator operators, drivers, general workers etc.)
    • Vessel Spot Marketing charter part negotiation, handling marine insurance and claims
    • Technical managements services to worldwide shipowners, planning, designing, supervising complex new building projects with stringent class, regulatory, safety, environmental and technical requirements
